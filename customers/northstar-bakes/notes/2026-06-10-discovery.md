# Discovery Call: Northstar Bakes

Date: 2026-06-10

## Attendees

- Maya Clarke, Head of Digital Operations
- Owen Patel, Finance Manager
- Consultant

## Notes

Northstar Bakes is preparing for its autumn subscription launch. Maya is worried that the team only knows whether a campaign worked two or three days after launch because sales data is exported manually.

Owen wants better margin visibility by kit type. Current spreadsheet reporting often mixes wholesale and ecommerce channels.

## Signals

- Strong interest in dashboards if they can be built without replacing Shopify.
- Cost sensitivity is moderate. The customer will fund a proof of concept if it avoids a large upfront commitment.
- They like the idea of starting with S3, Athena, and QuickSight.

## Follow-Up

- Send a one-page analytics proof-of-concept plan.
- Include an estimated monthly AWS cost range.
- Show how the first phase avoids storing payment data.

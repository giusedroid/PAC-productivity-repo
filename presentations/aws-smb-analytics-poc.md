# Slide Outline: SMB Analytics Proof of Concept

## 1. Why Now

SMB teams often have the data they need, but it is trapped in exports, spreadsheets, and SaaS tools.

## 2. First Workload

Start with a narrow reporting question that matters commercially.

## 3. Proposed AWS Pattern

S3, Glue, Athena, and QuickSight.

## 4. Success Criteria

- Data available within one business day
- Dashboard answers a defined business question
- Manual reporting time reduced
- No sensitive payment data included

## 5. Next Step

Two-week proof of concept using historical exports.

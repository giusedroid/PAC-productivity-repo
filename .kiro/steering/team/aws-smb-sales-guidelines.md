# Team Steering: AWS SMB Sales Guidelines

This mock team steering file represents manager-controlled guidance for exercises.

## Sales Motion

- Discover the business trigger before proposing architecture.
- Connect technical recommendations to revenue, resilience, speed, or cost control.
- For SMB customers, prefer phased adoption over large transformation programs.
- Identify a first valuable workload that can prove AWS adoption safely.

## Recommended First-Wave Patterns

- Static web hosting and CDN modernization
- Managed database migration
- Backup and disaster recovery
- Analytics foundation with S3 and QuickSight
- Serverless internal workflow automation
- IoT ingestion with managed streaming and time-series storage

## Guardrails

- Do not store real customer data in this repository.
- Do not commit credentials, recordings, or raw transcripts.
- Keep automation local-first unless a specific exercise says otherwise.
